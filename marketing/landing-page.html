Frequently Asked Questions

Is BidShield estimating software? ⌄

No. BidShield is a quality assurance tool—a pre-flight checklist for bids you've already prepared. You create the estimate. BidShield checks your work before you submit. Think of it as a second set of eyes that never gets tired.

How do I use it with my current workflow? ⌄

BidShield integrates into your existing process. Prepare your bid as you normally do. Before you submit, upload it to BidShield, run the checks, fix any gaps, and send it out. No software replacement. No retraining your team. Just a checklist step.

What if I only do a few bids per month? ⌄

Even one caught gap per month pays for the whole year. At $149/month, you're investing about $12 per bid if you do 12 bids per month. Most of our customers make that back on the first catch. The real question is: can you afford NOT to use it?

Do you store my bid data? ⌄

Yes. Your bid history stays in your account so you can track trends and see which checks catch issues most often. All data is encrypted and secured. You can export or delete your data anytime.

Can my whole team use one account? ⌄

Absolutely. Add team members to your account and set permissions. Solo estimators use one account. Larger departments can add multiple users and see unified reporting across all team bids.

Is there a free trial? ⌄

Yes. You get 14 days free, full access, no credit card required. Run as many bids through the checklist as you want. If you love it, upgrade. If not, no charge.
