## 🎯 **The Business Model Breakdown**

### 1️⃣ **LEARNING CENTER** (`/learning`)
**Purpose:** FREE educational content to attract visitors and build trust
**Monetization:** Leads visitors to paid products/courses
**Content Type:** Articles, guides, tutorials

**9 Learning Topics:**
1. Finding Work - How to find construction leads
2. Roof Measurement - Aerial measurement techniques
3. Reading Plans & Specs - Blueprint reading
4. Roofing Systems - 9 different roof types (TPO, EPDM, Metal, etc.)
5. Estimating Best Practices - Pro tips and workflows
6. Software & Technology - Tools comparison
7. Proposal Writing - How to write winning bids
8. Business Operations - Insurance, OSHA, licensing
9. Submittals & Shop Drawings - Documentation

**Each learning page has:**
- ✅ Free content preview
- 💰 Link to related PAID product/course at bottom

---

**Purpose:** PAID in-depth video training programs
**Price Range:** $147 - $497 per course

| Course | Price | Description |

| **AutoCAD Submittals** | $147 | Creating submittals in AutoCAD |
| **Estimating Software** | $97 | Software comparison & selection guide |
| **SketchUp Visualization** | $127 | 3D visualization for proposals |

**Skill Level Pages:**
- `/courses/beginner` - Filtered view of beginner courses

**User Journey:**
1. User finds FREE learning content

3. Clicks "Enroll Now" → Gumroad checkout
4. Pays one-time fee → Gets lifetime access

### 4️⃣ **MEMBERSHIP** (`/membership`)
**Purpose:** ALL-ACCESS subscription (Netflix model)
**Price:** $197/month OR $1,970/year (save $394)
**What's Included:**
- ✅ All 7 courses (worth $1,500)
- ✅ All 17 products (worth $800+)
- ✅ Monthly live Q&A sessions
- ✅ Private member community
- ✅ New content monthly
- ✅ Priority support

**Target Customer:**
- Serious estimators who want everything
- Companies training multiple employees
- Contractors building estimating department

**User Journey:**
1. User sees multiple courses/products they want
2. Realizes buying individually costs $2,000+
3. Sees membership = $197/mo for EVERYTHING
4. Subscribes via Gumroad
5. Gets instant access to full library
